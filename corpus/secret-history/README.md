# Secret History Corpus

Secret History is materialized as a full Apocalypse spine. It tracks hidden inheritance, myth, ideology, imagination, and social memory as forces that become operational in modern crisis.

Most Secret History chapters belong to Apocalypse by default. sh-11, sh-16, sh-17, and sh-18 also support the Civilization literary and imagination spine, with sh-16 serving as the routed Tolstoy endpoint for the Homer-to-Tolstoy corridor.
