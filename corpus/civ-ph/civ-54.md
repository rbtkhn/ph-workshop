This chapter gives the German side of the rival-modernities frame. Prussia, Konigsberg, education, reform, Bismarck, Nietzsche, Wagner, and Hitler are gathered around the idea of unity of will: the longing to overcome fragmentation through collective purpose.

## Reading Posture
