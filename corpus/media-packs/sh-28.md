# Secret History #28: Pax Judaica Media Pack

## How To Use This Pack

Use this pack before reading the transcript or commentary. It gives students a visual and documentary doorway into the lecture while keeping source claims, interpretation, and rights status separate. This is a curated draft awaiting human search and curation.

## Entry Object

### 01. Bretton Woods conference photograph

- **Type:** document
- **Source:** https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/Category:Bretton_Woods_Conference
- **Rights:** external_link_only
- **What to notice:** Bretton Woods imagery anchors the lecture's finance and empire claims in a real institutional moment.
- **Limit:** A conference photograph does not prove later claims about financial control.

## Context Anchors

### 04. ARPANET logical map
