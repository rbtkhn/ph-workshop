# Great Books Corpus

This corpus holds public source pointers for Predictive History Volume V: Great Books. It is active and in review for `gb-01` through `gb-09`.

The corpus entries are not substitutes for the source videos, Part I transcripts, or Part II commentary. They provide traceable routing from each source ID into the book chapter files, commentary surface, and PH-CIV orientation entry.

## Current Contract

- Public source IDs use `gb-01` through `gb-09`.
- Transfer book-track IDs such as `gb-ch01` are preserved only as metadata.
- Part I transcript bodies preserve the transferred lecture body exactly after normalization.
- Part II commentary is Great Books-native and in review.
- PH-CIV entries provide orientation and re-entry, not duplicate commentary.
