# Volume VI: Secret History

Volume VI is the Secret History spine of the World War semester. It reads hidden inheritance, myth, ideology, and imagination as operational forces in modern conflict.

## Dual-Role Nodes

sh-11, sh-16, sh-17, and sh-18 also support the Civilization literary and imagination spine. sh-16 is the routed Tolstoy endpoint for the Homer-to-Tolstoy corridor, with the caveat that it is not a dedicated Tolstoy lecture.
